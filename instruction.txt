This is a simple Quiz Game developed in Python by Vinay Pratap Singh

Simply to play this game, you have to select the Start Game option and have to choose the topic upon which you want to give the Quiz

After selecting the topic you will get the question and will have 4 option as MCQ
Simply select the correct option and hit enter

If the entered answer will be wrong, you will get the correct answer there and in final you will get the total correct answers you have given.
